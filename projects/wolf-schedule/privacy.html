Wolf Schedule Privacy Policy

It is Wolf Schedule's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website. This Privacy Policy applies to the Wolf Schedule Google Chrome extension (hereinafter, "us", "we", "the application") available for download on the Chrome Web Store.

Collected Information

Wolf Schedule collects non-personally-identifying information that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Wolf Schedule's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how our users use the application. This information is kept internally and is not released publicly.

Wolf Schedule also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. We also do not release this information.

Information We Share and Disclose

Wolf Schedule is developed, maintained, and distributed by a private individual. The extension is independent from any educational district, agency, or institution such as the Houston Independent School District and Westside High School.

We will not disclose any information upon request. This is with the highly unlikely exception of a law, regulation, legal process, or official governmental request in order to protect the safety of a person and/or to protect the safety or integrity of our platform.

Aggregated Statistics

Wolf Schedule may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. Wolf Schedule does not disclose this information publicly.

Advertisements/Sponsored Material

In the event of the addition of advertisements/sponsored content: Ads appearing on our extension may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. Our privacy policy is independent of such Ads.

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Wolf Schedule uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

By continuing to use our application, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Wolf Schedule's use of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
